# Tucker&rsquo;s Humans

A reverse of the _Dungeon Crawl_ for powerful characters based on the famous
_Tucker&rsquo;s Kobolds_ from [_Dragon
127_](https://media.wizards.com/2014/downloads/dnd/TuckersKobolds.pdf), a
popular tournament dungeon updated for D&amp;D 5e. _This_ adventure plays
characters as the defenders.
