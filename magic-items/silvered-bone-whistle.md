# Silvered Bone Whistle

_Wondrous Item, varies (requires attunement by a Delegate)

This item is given to all players in the "Oh no, here come the Humans!"
one-shot adventure. See the **[Warren
Commander](./feats/warren-commander.md)** feat.

Blow this whistle with an
[Action](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/rules-glossary#Action),
[Bonus
Action](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/rules-glossary#BonusAction),
or
[Reaction](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/rules-glossary#Reaction).
Call an allied Kobold squad to your side in their warren within 60 feet. While
you have
[Attunement](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/rules-glossary#Attunement)
to this whistle, you have
[Advantage](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/rules-glossary#Advantage)
for
[initiative](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/rules-glossary#Initiative).
